We have moved to our new place at 70 Attfield Street Maddington

Published On: March 14, 2017

Home >> News >> We have moved to our new place at 70 Attfield Street Maddington

Granada Medical Practice was established by Dr Arosha Vidyabhushana and Dr Fatin Wajdi on the 16th of January 2012, beginning as a small practice on Burslem Drive, Maddington. At that point in time, Dr Arosha and Dr Fatin had been working together in the Maddington area since 2008, and welcomed their ever-growing patient base on the new and exciting chapter of their careers. Today, the practice is open seven days a week and only being closed during public holidays.

“We are blessed with very efficient and friendly staff .I believes the success so far with all aspects is largely due to the commitment of our practice manager, nurses and the reception staff. Also we are very proud to have extremely experienced and commited doctors who are well respected and loved by the patients of the area”

- Practice Principal GP Dr Arosha Vidyabhushana.

Patient feedback plays a vital role in maintaining the high standard of healthcare that Granada Medical Practice sets for itself, and so patients are always welcome to provide their feedback on the services they receive from both admin staff and the medical staff through the feedback questionnaire available at the front desk. In Dr Arosha’s words, “it is the best way to improve the services and share the satisfaction of the good services we provide with all our staff!”.
