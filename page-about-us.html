About Granada Medical Practice

Granada Medical is situated in Maddington, Western Australia. Our practice is located approximately 13 kilometres away from Perth CBD. We are a predominately bulk billing general practice but a gap fee may apply for some procedures. You will be informed of any gap fee at the time of booking or prior to the consultation.

We provide a wide range of general practice services to the community and our staff are dedicated to provide quality care in a friendly and welcoming environment.
